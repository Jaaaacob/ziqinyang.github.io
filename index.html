As an undergraduate at Wuhan University, I am passionate about applying artificial intelligence (AI) and computer vision to advance intelligent systems, particularly in autonomous driving and geospatial analytics.

Autonomous Driving: Focused on sensor fusion (LiDAR, cameras), 3D object detection, and real-time decision-making algorithms.

Computer Vision: Specialized in deep learning for semantic segmentation, SLAM, and robust environmental perception systems.

Geospatial Intelligence: Leveraging remote sensing (satellite/hyperspectral imagery) and surveying techniques for high-precision mapping and terrain modeling.

I aim to integrate AI-driven solutions with geospatial data to address challenges in autonomous navigation and sustainable smart-city development.
